Conway's Game of Life

A cellular automaton devised by John Conway in 1970

10 fps
30 x 30

Generation: 0

Population: 0

Rules:

  1. Any live cell with fewer than 2 neighbors dies (underpopulation)
  2. Any live cell with 2 or 3 neighbors lives on
  3. Any live cell with more than 3 neighbors dies (overpopulation)
  4. Any dead cell with exactly 3 neighbors becomes a live cell (reproduction)

Click on cells to toggle them alive/dead
